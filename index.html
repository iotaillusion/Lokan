About Us

Lokan is dedicated to bringing safe, reliable, and human-centered AI into everyday life. We believe technology should protect the people who use it, not exploit them. Every product we create is built with security and privacy as first requirements, not afterthoughts.

Our mission is to make smart home technology accessible and affordable. We help people convert their existing houses into smart homes without sacrificing privacy or safety. Our solutions are designed to be easy to set up, intuitive to use, and respectful of user data.

We prioritize local processing where possible, strict data minimization, and clear controls so users always know what their devices are doing. By combining security best practices with modern design, we set a higher standard for what home AI can do to empower homeowners to take control of their spaces with confidence.
