Our Process

We follow a rigorous four-step process to ensure your home AI is safe, effective, and tailored to your needs.

1. Assess

Audit devices and network to map the ecosystem and risks.

2. Configure

Define least‑privilege policies and automation rules.

3. Deploy

Install hub. Run models locally. Use cloud only for non‑sensitive bursts.

4. Verify

Expose dashboards and auditable logs for full oversight.
